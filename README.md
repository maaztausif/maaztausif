## 📱 Featured Apps

### [Miplanit – To-Do Lists & Tasks](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/miplanit-to-do-lists-tasks/id1551807583)
Calendar sync across Apple, Google, and Microsoft. Users can create events, manage shopping and to-do lists, and attach purchase receipts. Serverless backend using AWS Lambda and Python.

### [IBIZI](https://apps.apple.com/pk/app/ibizi/id1659590288)
Car dealership platform for the US market. Browse listings, buy and sell vehicles, and track car performance across multiple metrics.

### [TopCity-1](https://apps.apple.com/pk/app/topcity-1/id1619949970)
Society management app that connects residents and owners. Owners get full visibility over tenants and can manage home-related issues directly from the app.

### [Qarya](https://apps.apple.com/pk/app/qarya/id1640047441)
End-to-end solution for residential societies. Handles maintenance requests, plumbing issues, and all day-to-day problems that arise within a community.

### [Smox Trimsetters](https://apps.apple.com/de/app/smoxtrimsetters/id1602527269?l=en-GB)
Barber shop app with an integrated e-commerce module. Barbers can sell products directly through the app with ShipEngine handling delivery logistics.

### [Smox Pro](https://apps.apple.com/de/app/smox-pro/id1455895656?l=en-GB)
Companion app to Smox Trimsetters. Booking, scheduling, and shop management for barbers and their clients.

### SocioMee
Social platform where I built the Stories module — a hybrid of Snapchat and Instagram Stories. Included camera capture, media overlays, timed display, and swipe navigation built from scratch in Swift.
